About

This is one of those websites people use to document for themselves and others how to solve specific computer-related problems. I have had much use of such personal websites through the years, especially before the days of Stack Exchange. So I think it is time for me to give something back to the community. I will try to make a post now and then when I solve something I could not find on Stack Exchange.

The site may also contain one or two longer posts about various themes. A sudden urge to write some ramblings about computational workflows was actually the reason I finally got around to publishing this site. I guess this topic appeals to my neatfreak tendency.
